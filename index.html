About
Me

Hello! I am a person of many passions. As a lifelong animal-lover, I pursued a degree in equine husbandry and eventually started working with cats and dogs as a veterinary assistant. After I realized I was devoting much of my free time towards writing code for video game mods, I took the leap in 2020 and enrolled in UNC's Coding Boot Camp. As an entry-level full-stack web developer, I am looking forward to blending my new technical knowledge with the skills I've built through years of working in customer service and with animals. No matter the industry, I feel attention to detail, flexibility, and compassion are paramount to any project or client interaction.
